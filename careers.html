JOB SUMMARY:

RRAN (Revitalising Rainfed Agriculture Network) is engaged in working towards relevant and effective policies, programs and increased public investments for rainfed agriculture to meet the development aspirations of people and needs of the ecosystem.

A decade long RRAN is a pan India network of individuals, civil society, researchers, bankers, and those serving various institutions but are interested in the agenda. It works across rainfed areas of different states in partnership with partner organizations and State Governments / Departments in furthering evidence for evolving effective and relevant policies. The network works across the domains of practice, policy and research.

Thematically, the network engages with a natural resource integrated perspective on soils, water, crop systems, livestock, fisheries, markets and livelihoods.

The Network is operated through its two arms, Network Hub (engaged with awareness generation, networking, synthesizing experiences and evolving the programs) and the Project Management Unit (PMU) – principally involved in managing the projects evolved in the network.

RRA network is expanding its team servicing the network. We are looking for a young professional to be placed in the Bundelkhand region of Central India to ensure strengthening of the rainfed agenda among the rainfed regions of Bundelkhand. It requires extensive travel to some of the interior locations & establishing networking beyond the Bundelkhand region.

RRAN is an equal opportunity employer. Female candidates are encouraged to apply.
