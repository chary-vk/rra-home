Communications Specialist – RRA Network Hub

BASE LOCATION:

Hyderabad, Telangana.

REPORTING TO:

National Coordinator and Senior Coordinator, RRA Network

JOB SUMMARY:

We are looking for an experienced, passionate and creative Communications Specialist to join our team full time. In this role, one of your core responsibilities will be to work to strengthen our media outreach efforts and increase our online presence and improve RRA Network’s recognition in the public by working closely with our Communications Officer.

The Communications Specialist will need to create and maintain a strategy for enabling an inclusive, regular, and decentralized system of communication that showcases the work of our thematic groups and state/regional networks. The ideal candidate would have strong interpersonal skills to collaborate with a large, diverse group of people as well as be able to work autonomously, defining one’s targets and achieving them.

In addition to the responsibilities outlined below, the Communications Specialist should be prepared, as and when necessary, to take any additional related responsibilities as determined by his/her supervisors.

● Help in content writing and support copy/design edits for the website.
