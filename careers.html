JOB SUMMARY:

The rainfed areas of India are the most variable and unpredictable environments for agriculture. Yet, over generations, communities living in this area have learnt how to harness this variability to support their economies, societies, and agroecosystems, carefully breeding livestock and varieties of crops that can thrive in these areas. RRA Network, spread across 11 states in India, in collaboration with the farmers, validates this indigenous knowledge through action-research projects and uses it for formulating enabling policies to revitalize rainfed agriculture.

RRA Network seeks to hire a suitable candidate for a Research Associate position to conduct research, compile evidence, and create documentation that will drive change to revitalize rainfed agriculture systems. We are looking for a motivated researcher with a critical mindset to evaluate and prioritize research needs, and the social skills to work with the diverse members of the network.

The successful applicant will assist the team members in undertaking robust research and analysis. She/he will have to travel to different locations on a need basis. Performance will be primarily assessed on documentation outputs. In addition to the duties and responsibilities outlined below, the Research Associate should be prepared, as and when necessary, to take on additional responsibilities to help the team to respond to any unforseen developments.
